TRESTLE WATCH CO.

Trestle Watch Company is an American brand founded by a watch lover and history enthusiast. I grew up loving the World War 2 era and combined my love for watches and history. Seeing that most watches based off of that time period were either too clunky-looking or just too out of date and unfashionable, it was simple to me. Create a watch with a vintage feel, yet modern and slim. From that mindset, Trestle Watch Company was born.

MW POLAR FOODS.

MW Polar is a distributor of canned foods based in Norwalk, Ca. They began their venture in 1976, and since then has expanded into retail stores all across the United States. I was hired as the Digital Marketing Intern to help bring the company into the internet age.

  • Graphic Designer
  • Digital Marketer

LIES CLOTHING CO.

Lies Clothing Co. was a streetwear brand known for its satire on world affairs. Its own brand name was to mock the world we live in, a world of lies and deceit. Many of its designs were political in nature to bring awareness to the youth of the injustice among them.
