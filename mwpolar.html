MW POLAR FOODS

MW Polar is a distributor of canned foods based in Norwalk, Ca. They began their venture in 1976, and since then has expanded into retail stores all across the United States. I was hired as A Digital Marketer and Graphic Designer to help bring the company into the internet age.

STORY

In 2015, I extended my marketing and design experience into the food industry. I was hired to breathe life into not only their social media accounts, but their entire internet presence.

CHALLENGES

  • Company had no presence online with an already low brand recognition.
  • Company's social media had little to no following with little engagement
  • Social Media accounts filled with poor content and unattractive designs.
  • Website was not up-to-date and had poor design with uninteresting content.
  • Product designs were not up-to-date which were similar to product styles from the 70's.

SOLUTIONS

  • Created a Sample Review Program for customers to try products and leave reviews.
  • Grew the company's social media to where customers anticipate news and engage with.
  • Social media posts became more lively with exciting news and aesthetically pleasing graphics.
  • Completely redesigned the website to a modern aesthetic as well as placement of effective call-to-actions.
  • Improved old labels using design and marketing structure to attract consumers and wholesale buyers.
