TRESTLE WATCH CO.

Trestle Watch Company is an American brand founded by a watch lover and history enthusiast. I grew up loving the World War 2 era and combined my love for watches and history. Seeing that most watches based off of that time period were either too clunky-looking or just too out of date and unfashionable, it was simple to me. Create a watch with a vintage feel, yet modern and slim. From that mindset, Trestle Watch Company was born.

STORY

In August of 2016, I designed and created a brand of watches. I was able to surpass the funding goal on Kickstarter and brought my project to life.

CHALLENGES

  • No social media following.
  • No website or any web presence.
  • No existing way for consumers to contact the brand.
  • No actual knowledge of horology.
  • Never used Kickstarter or any crowdfunding site.
  • Starting capital of a college student.
  • $8500.00 needed for first round of inventory

SOLUTIONS

  • Launched social platforms ahead to attract interested customers.
  • Created website and locked-in reviews and "shout-outs" from bloggers/influencers.
  • Connected business email with existing personal email: info@trestlewatch.com
  • Researched which selling points sold and implemented them into the product.
  • Researched data and implemented ways for successful Kickstarter project.
  • Used own capital from part-time jobs to fund prototypes.
  • Surpassed the funding goal and reached $20,000.
